About Us

GSSU concentrates primarily on Air Rifle shooting, and falls under the national governing body, SANNSU
(Suid Afrikaanse Nasionale Skole Skutters Unie)



Inside of Gauteng we have participants mainly from the following schools/clubs:

  • Florida

  • Linden

  • Helpmekaar

  • Randburg
